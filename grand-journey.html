🗒 Europe Planning Tips

  • Berlin Reichstag dome: Free but must pre-register at bundestag.de — do this from Auckland. Very popular, often booked out 2–3 weeks ahead.
  • Berlin → Munich ICE train: Book at bahn.de — early booking saves up to 50%. Direct trains run ~hourly, journey ~4.5hrs.
  • Rental car (Munich): Collect Day 3 at Munich Airport (MUC) or city. Your NZ international driving permit is valid throughout Europe. Check whether your NZ licence needs an IDP — it does in some EU countries.
  • Toll vignettes: Austria, Switzerland and Czechia require motorway sticker (~€35 total). Buy at the border crossing or order online before you go.
  • Italy ZTL zones: Florence, Rome and Venice all have restricted driving zones in their historic centres. Always park on the outskirts and walk/use public transport inside.
  • Vatican & Anne Frank House: Both need advance booking — essential in peak summer. Book from Auckland before you leave.
  • Lourdes TGV: Peak pilgrimage season June–July. Book immediately after booking accommodation — trains fill weeks out.
  • Bastille Day (14 Jul): You'll be in Paris the evening of 13 Jul — fireworks at the Eiffel Tower that night. Magical and completely unplanned! Book the Paris→Amsterdam Thalys for 14 Jul well in advance as it's a national holiday.

🌴 Sri Lanka Planning Tips

  • ETA Visa: NZ passport holders need an Electronic Travel Authorisation — apply at eta.gov.lk before departure. ~USD $20, instant approval.
  • Private driver: Strongly recommended for the Galle/Mirissa/Kandy loop. ~USD $60–80/day, far easier than self-driving on Sri Lankan roads. Your Colombo hotel can arrange.
  • July weather: SW monsoon affects the south and west coasts — expect short tropical showers, not all-day rain. Light rain jacket recommended. Kandy and hill country are generally clear.
  • Temple of the Sacred Tooth (Kandy): One of Buddhism's most sacred sites. Dress code: shoulders and knees covered. Arrive at puja ceremony times (6am, 9:30am, 6:30pm) for the full experience.
  • Whale watching (Mirissa): Blue whales Jun–Aug, high success rate in July. Book the evening before for a 6am departure. Takes ~3hrs.
  • Return flight CMB → AKL: Best options — Singapore Airlines CMB→SIN→AKL or Emirates CMB→DXB→AKL. Depart morning of 24 Jul to arrive AKL same evening. Confirm 24 Jul arrival when booking.
  • Currency: Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR). USD accepted widely in tourist areas. ATMs available in Colombo, Galle and Kandy.
